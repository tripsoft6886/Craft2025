About Roly Poly

Our café is Da Nang's first specialty fresh spring roll restaurant, dedicated to handmade spring rolls crafted to order with high-quality ingredients. We offer vegan and gluten-free options. Looking for restaurants in An Thuong, the My An area, or a Vietnamese café in Da Nang for healthy food near My Khe Beach? Our cozy, Instagrammable vintage café offers indoor seating with free Wi-Fi. Perfect for a healthy breakfast, brunch, lunch, light snack, or dinner. We serve each spring roll with our unique house-made sauces. Enjoy them with our signature fresh juices, specialty Vietnamese salted coffee, coconut coffee, or premium matcha latte.
