About Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang

Roly Poly Fresh Spring Rolls Da Nang is the city’s first specialty café dedicated to fresh spring rolls, rice paper rolls, and summer rolls — all handmade to order with high-quality ingredients. We offer vegan-friendly and gluten-free options, perfect for health-conscious travelers.

Located in the heart of An Thuong, just minutes from My Khe Beach, our cozy vintage café is a favorite with locals and tourists looking for healthy Vietnamese food. Dine in and enjoy free Wi-Fi, indoor seating, and Instagram-worthy vibes.

Each roll is served with our unique house-made sauces. Pair your meal with our signature drinks: Vietnamese salted coffee, coconut coffee, matcha lattes, and refreshing juices.
